Sandwich

Description

You may think that this is a simple sandwich but in simplicity lies the beauty of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Baloney
  • Lettuce
  • Mozzarela cheese slice

Steps

  1. Put the baloney on a slice of bread.
  2. Put the lettuce on the baloney.
  3. Put the mozzarela cheese on the lettuce.
  4. put the second slice of bread on the mozzarela slice.
  5. eat.
