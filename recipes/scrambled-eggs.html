Scrambled eggs

Description

Now here is a thing of beauty the breakfast of millions around the world

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Olive oil or regular oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Put olive oil on the cooking pan (enough so the eegs wont stick).
  2. Break both eggs and put them in the cooking pan that has the oil.
  3. Stir with a spoon until they are done.
  4. Eat.
